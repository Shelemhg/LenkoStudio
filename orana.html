Orana

What It Does

Orana helps creators optimize posting times, formats, and captions using lightweight insights. It focuses on non-sensitive, testable variables and operates in a research-friendly mode that emphasizes transparency and control.

How Orana Treats Your Data

  • Data sources: your authorized connections (e.g., TikTok OAuth) or publicly available pages where permitted.
  • Privacy first: tokens are stored securely; you can disconnect at any time.
  • Research mode: any sensitive/suggestiveness metrics are used only for correlation analysis on adult-only datasets.
  • We share only de-identified or aggregated insights where possible.
