About Us

Our Story

Lenko Studio, a distinct style. From the busy streets of Manhattan to Upstate New York, Lenko Studio began with a different concept. We later moved to Mexico and evolved, offering a service shaped by the style and experiences of the world’s capital. Deep emotions, rich colors, and naturally elegant posing are the signature of our work.

Lenko Studio Weddings. Every service is built on a close, attentive relationship with our clients to ensure expectations are exceeded. We preserve one of life’s most important moments in a relaxed and natural way—telling your story from beginning to end while we capture the highlights and the small, meaningful details of your day. These memories are crafted to be treasures for a lifetime.

Approach & Values

  • Calm direction: Relaxed sessions that keep you present and comfortable.
  • Editorial color: Rich, cinematic tones with natural light whenever possible.
  • Story first: Honest storytelling that balances iconic moments and subtle details.
  • Close collaboration: We plan with you so expectations aren’t just met—they’re exceeded.
  • Mexico City roots, global reach: Based in CDMX; we happily travel.

What We Offer

  • Weddings: From proposals and “Save the Date” to ceremony coverage and “Trash the Dress”.
  • Portraits: Editorial portraits for individuals, couples, and teams.
  • Projects: Brand films and visual stories with cinematic polish.
